## What Workers Comp Covers in Upstate NY

### 1. Medical Expenses
Workers' compensation pays 100% of necessary and reasonable medical treatment costs for covered work injuries, including:
- Emergency room visits
- Doctor and hospital care
- Prescription medications
- Physical therapy

### 2. Wage Replacement
Workers' comp provides 2/3 of an employee's average weekly earnings (up to the state maximum) for temporary/indefinite disability claims.

### 3. Vocational Rehabilitation
Coverage assists injured employees in returning to suitable employment through retraining programs.

### 4. Death Benefits
Dependents receive financial support in the case of work-related fatalities.
