## FAQ: Healthcare Insurance in NY

**Q: How much malpractice insurance do I need?**
A: Depends on your specialty and contracts. Most hospitals require $1-3 million. Surgeons typically need $5 million+.

**Q: What is tail coverage?**
A: It extends coverage after a claims-made policy ends. Required if you retire or switch carriers.

**Q: Do I need cyber insurance if I have EHR?**
A: Yes. Electronic records increase breach risk. HIPAA requires breach notification, which cyber insurance covers.

**Q: Can I get insurance for telemedicine?**
A: Yes. Most malpractice carriers now cover telemedicine, but disclose it to your carrier.
