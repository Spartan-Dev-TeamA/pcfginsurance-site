# Why Syracuse Businesses Should Use an Independent Insurance Agent

When it comes to business insurance, you have a choice: go directly to one insurance company (captive agent) or work with an independent insurance agency that represents multiple carriers.

If you're like most Syracuse business owners, you probably didn't even know there was a difference. But that difference could save you hundreds—maybe thousands—every year.

## What Is an Independent Insurance Agent?

An independent insurance agent represents multiple insurance companies, not just one. This means they can shop your coverage across different carriers to find the best rate and coverage combination.

**Captive agent:** Works for one insurance company — can only sell you that company's products

**Independent agent:** Works for you, not the insurance company — can shop multiple carriers to find what fits

### 5. Advocacy When You Need It
When you have a claim, we advocate for you. We're not just a policy number—we're your partner in protecting your business.
