## FAQ: Retail Insurance in NY

**Q: Do I need product liability if I only resell products?**
A: Yes. Anyone who sells a product can be held liable for defects.

**Q: Can I use my personal auto insurance for delivery?**
A: No. Personal auto excludes business use. You need commercial auto.

**Q: How much property insurance do I need?**
A: Enough to replace everything—building, inventory, equipment. Conduct a full inventory.

**Q: What if I'm also online (e-commerce)?**
A: You need both retail and e-commerce coverage. Cyber insurance is essential.
