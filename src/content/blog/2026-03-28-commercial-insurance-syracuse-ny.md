## Essential Commercial Insurance for Syracuse Businesses

### Workers' Compensation

**Required by NY law.**

Syracuse employers must carry workers' comp for all employees. Industries like manufacturing and healthcare have higher rates.

**Average cost:** $1.50-3.00 per $100 of payroll

---

### General Liability

**Protects against:**

- Customer injuries
- Property damage to others
- Advertising injury

**Recommended limits:** $1-2 million

---

### Commercial Property

**Protects:**

- Your building (if you own)
- Equipment
- Inventory
- Furniture

**Key consideration:** Syracuse winters require proper building maintenance to prevent ice dam damage and collapsed roofs.

---

### Commercial Auto

**Required if you have business vehicles.**

Personal auto policies don't cover business use. Delivery and service businesses especially need this.

## Flood Insurance in Syracuse

### Onondaga Lake Area

Many areas near Onondaga Lake are in flood zones. Standard property insurance doesn't cover floods.

### NFIP Coverage

National Flood Insurance Program:
- Building: Up to $250,000
- Contents: Up to $100,000

**Important:** There's a 30-day waiting period for flood insurance to take effect.
