## What Determines Your Cost?

Several factors affect what you'll pay:

### 1. Your Industry
A construction company on Syracuse's north side pays more than a consulting firm downtown. High-risk industries (construction, manufacturing, food service) see higher premiums.

### 2. Business Size & Revenue
More employees and higher revenue means more exposure. Insurers use your payroll and gross receipts to calculate premiums.

### 3. Claims History
If you've filed claims in the past, expect higher rates. A clean loss history keeps costs down.

### 4. Coverage Limits
Higher liability limits cost more but protect your business better. We recommend at least $1M per occurrence for general liability.

### 5. Location
Syracuse businesses in flood zones or areas with higher crime rates pay more for property coverage.

## Cost-Saving Tips for Syracuse Businesses

### Bundle with a BOP
Combining property, liability, and business interruption into a Business Owner's Policy typically saves 10-20% versus buying separately.

### Increase Your Deductible
Going from $500 to $1,000 deductible can reduce your premium by 15-25%.

### Implement Safety Programs
Workers' compensation insurers offer discounts for safety training, return-to-work programs, and drug-free workplace certifications.
